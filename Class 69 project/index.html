Inter School Football Competition
Came second in Inter School Football Competition

On 23 August, 2019, the Sub-Junior Boys Football team of NPSI Mysore had the opportunity to participate in the under-10 tournament organized by NAFL North School in Bangalore. Students of classes 3 to 5 took part in the tournament. The 16 boys who participated in the tournament, played with tact, skill and right strategy to enter the semi finals. Children were excited to wear the new jerseys tailor made for the tournament with the School logo and name. This experience truly enhanced their team spirit. Children had a great exposure and a learning experience as this was the pilot team from the Sub-Junior category, which took part in a tournament outside School.

Hackathon
Participated in Hackathon

Nine students from classes 9,11 and 12 participated in the Hackathon held at NPS Koramangala on the 20th of August 2019. The students were required to build an software application which would be new, innovative and would bring about a positive change in the society. The atmosphere were thrilling and all the participants were extremely enthusiastic and looked forward to start coding! 8 hours of coding and brain storming helped them in developing logical thinking, time management skills and increase their knowledge in programming and technology. The students were guided by mentors from IBM, who helped them to develop their codes in a more efficient manner. Their expertise in the subject was a trait to look upto. It was an experience that will be cherished by all of the young 'young coders'! They are extremely grateful for this wonderful opportunity which helped them strengthen their skills and inspired them to dive further into the world of coding!
